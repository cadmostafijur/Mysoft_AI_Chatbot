MYSOFT HEAVEN (BD) LTD. — PRODUCTS & PLATFORMS
=================================================

In addition to custom development, Mysoft Heaven (BD) Ltd. has built several proprietary products and SaaS platforms.

1. MYSOFT ERP
--------------
A comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning solution designed for small and medium-sized businesses in Bangladesh.
- Modules: Accounting, HR & Payroll, Inventory, Sales, Purchase, Manufacturing
- Deployment: Cloud-based (SaaS) or on-premise
- Pricing: Starts from BDT 15,000/month for the basic plan
- Clients: 30+ companies actively using Mysoft ERP
- Key Features:
  - Multi-branch and multi-currency support
  - Bangla and English language interface
  - VAT and tax compliance for Bangladesh
  - Role-based access control
  - Real-time dashboards and reporting
  - Mobile app for on-the-go access

2. MYSOFT LMS (LEARNING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)
--------------------------------------------
An online education platform for schools, universities, and training institutes.
- Features: Course management, video lectures, quizzes, assignments, certificates
- Live class integration with Zoom and Google Meet
- Student progress tracking and analytics
- Parent portal for K-12 institutions
- Pricing: BDT 8,000/month for up to 500 students
- Clients: 15+ educational institutions across Bangladesh

3. MYSOFT HMS (HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)
--------------------------------------------
A digital health management platform for hospitals and clinics.
- Modules: Patient registration, appointment scheduling, electronic health records (EHR), billing, pharmacy, laboratory
- Telemedicine module for remote consultations
- Integration with diagnostic equipment
- HIPAA-compliant data security
- Pricing: Custom pricing based on hospital size
- Clients: 10+ hospitals and clinics

4. MYSOFT POS (POINT OF SALE)
-------------------------------
A modern point-of-sale system for retail stores and restaurants.
- Features: Barcode scanning, inventory tracking, sales analytics, customer loyalty programs
- Works offline with automatic sync when online
- Supports multiple payment methods (cash, card, mobile banking: bKash, Nagad)
- Pricing: BDT 3,000/month per terminal
- Clients: 50+ retail outlets and restaurants

5. SHOPBD — E-COMMERCE PLATFORM
---------------------------------
A white-label e-commerce platform for Bangladeshi businesses.
- Features: Product catalog, shopping cart, payment gateway integration (SSLCommerz, bKash), order management, delivery tracking
- SEO optimized with built-in analytics
- Multi-vendor marketplace support
- Pricing: BDT 5,000/month for starter plan
- Launched: 2022

6. MYSOFT HRMS (HUMAN RESOURCE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM)
---------------------------------------------------
A cloud-based HR management platform.
- Features: Employee database, attendance tracking (biometric integration), leave management, payroll processing, recruitment module
- Compliance with Bangladesh labor law
- Employee self-service portal
- Pricing: BDT 50/employee/month (minimum 20 employees)
- Clients: 25+ organizations
